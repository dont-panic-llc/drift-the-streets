    FAQ

    Frequently asked questions from Attendees, VIPs, Drivers, and Vendors.

    Attendee Questions

    What is Drifting?

    Drifting is when a driver throws their car sideways into a corner and maintains control while spinning the tires.

    Do you give a military discount?

    Yes! All active duty, reserve, and honorably discharged veterans of our military get free entry into the event. Valid Military ID or Discharge papers required.

    Can we smoke?

    We do not allow smoking inside of the event grounds.

    Can I bring a cooler?

    We are not allowing outside food or beverages inside of the event grounds. However, there will be plenty of affordable options inside of the event grounds.

    Driver Questions

    How much space will I have for my crew and trailer?

    You will have dedicated parking for your trailer and adequate space in the pits to work on your cars as you wish.

    What does my entry fee include?

    You will recieve free food and water for yourself, plus one, and discounted Pit Crew Rates, along with your race entry.

    When can I show up?

    You can show up anytime on Saturday between 8AM and 8PM. On Sunday, you will receive a schedule of the day's events in your driver packet after registration.

    VIP Questions

    What does the vip ticket include?

    You will get well over a three hundred dollar value, inc: a custom t-shirt, custom Hoodie, custom hat, and an event themed water bottle. Also you receive an exclusive VIP badge which will grant you access to the top sponsors and exclusive viewing areas.

    Where is the VIP area located?

    The VIP area is located on top of the Mill Street parking garage, you will enjoy the best views, great food, and get to meet drivers as they finish their racing!

    Do I have to stay in the VIP area the entire time?

    Absolutely not! You are free to roam about the event area as you wish and will even have limited access to shuttles carrying you from the garage to the paddock.

    Does my VIP Ticket include entrance to the post party?

    No, due to limited space, the post part is an additional upgrade to your VIP Ticket. More details on the post party will be available at a later date.

    Vendor Questions

    How big of a space do I get?

    Please E-mail us and we will send you a package containing the range of options available to you. However, our vendor sizes are either 10x10 ft or 20x20 ft . You can purchase multiple lots if you wish to have a large space.

    How many vendor spaces are available?

    We have over 100 available spaces for vendors, ranging from 10x10ft to 20x20ft spaces.

    When can I set up?

    If your lot is located on the paddock you may show up anytime Saturday morning, if you're elsewhere we request you show up late Saturday night or early Sunday morning. Contact us at Drift the Streets Info

    About the Competition

    A grassroots style event with a professional twist! Each driver will perform two single style runs to be scored, once all drivers have had their chances to run, the round will end. At the end of each round half of all drivers will be eliminated based on points received (better of two runs). This process will continue until we have crowned our grand champion!

    Competitive Prizes

    With a 3 thousand dollar grand prize for winning, there is a huge incentive to come have a great day of drifting! But don't worry second place will still receive 2 thousand dollars and third will receive a thousand dollar prize, so you won't have to finish in first to win!. We hope to see you in the pits on race day!
